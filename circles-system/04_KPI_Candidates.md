# KPI Candidates (Exploratory)

## Experience Quality
- CSAT (1–5)
- NPS (willingness to recommend)
- Subjective satisfaction with table experience

## Matching Effectiveness
- Placement rate (percentage of registered participants seated)
- Table completion rate (4–6 participants)
- Algorithm prediction vs post-event feedback alignment

## Discourse & Dynamics
- Self-reported conversation flow
- Perceived inclusion / voice balance
- Absence of dominance or social isolation signals

## Retention & Continuity
- Repeat attendance rate
- Direct connection rate (exchange of contact details)
- Post-event follow-up interactions

## Risk & Safety
- Incident or mismatch reports
- Subjective discomfort or exclusion feedback
- Early departure or no-show correlation

## Measurement Risks
- Politeness bias inflating positive scores
- High placement rates masking poor experience
- Averages hiding individual negative outliers

## Open Measurement Questions
- How to measure meaningful depth without intrusiveness?
- Does a connection count only if it continues beyond the event?
- Should KPI weighting differ between first-time and returning participants?
